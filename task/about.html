About Us

HAPPINESS PLANS is a young, dynamic and growing company delivering its services through creativity, culture and innovation. Offering full spectrum of destination programs ranging from luxury ecotourism experiences, wellness retreats, adventure exploration packages, family vacations and beach weddings. Our mission is to create unforgettable travel experiences that nurture the body, mind, and soul.

At HAPPINESS PLANS, we believe that travel should be more than just a way to escape the routine of daily life. We strive to create meaningful and authentic experiences that allow you to connect with the local culture and community. Whether you are looking to explore a new city, go on a safari, or simply relax on a beautiful beach, we have the expertise and resources to make your dream trip a reality.

Over rich experiences, we have established strong partnerships with local suppliers and hotels, allowing us to offer competitive pricing without compromising on quality. Our team is available around the clock to provide assistance and support before, during, and after your trip, ensuring a seamless and stress-free travel experience. Whether you are looking to explore a new destination, go on an adventure tour, or simply relax on a beautiful beach, HAPPINESS PLANS has the expertise and resources to make your dream trip a reality.
